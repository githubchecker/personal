## 🏆 SCHOOL 1: Birla High School for Boys, Moira Street

> *"The Ivy League of Kolkata — 84 Years of Producing Leaders"*

### 🎯 Identity & Legacy
Established in **1941** by the Late Shri Lakshmi Niwas Birla under the Vidya Mandir Society, Birla High School (BHS) is the oldest and most prestigious boys-only CBSE school in Kolkata. With a heritage of over 8 decades and an alumni network that reads like a who's-who of Indian finance, industry, and academia, this school carries enormous brand equity. There is also a **co-educational branch at Mukundapur** (South Kolkata) for families who need mixed schooling, but the Moira Street branch remains the flagship.

**Student Strength:** ~2,222 students | **Faculty:** ~186 teachers | **Ratio:** Approximately 1:12

- "Best School Chain of 2023" (Vidya Mandir Society) — New Delhi Award Ceremony

### 📚 CBSE Curriculum & Academic Depth

**Primary (KG–V):** Activity-based learning with subjects including Maths, Science, Computers, GK, and Value Education. Emphasis on conceptual understanding over rote learning.

**Middle School (VI–VIII):** Core subjects include English, Hindi/Bengali, Sanskrit/French/German/Mandarin (language choice), Mathematics, Science, History & Geography, IT, Physical Education, and Value Education. Specialization begins in Mathematics with advanced problem sets.

**Senior School (IX–X):** CBSE Board preparation. Strong Science and Mathematics track. The school is famous for producing the **highest number of 100/100 Math scorers in Kolkata** in CBSE boards, year after year.

**Senior Secondary (XI–XII):** Three streams — Science (PCM/PCB), Commerce, and Humanities. Science + Commerce are the most popular. The school feeds students directly into IITs, IIMs, CA programmes, and top foreign universities.

**Unique Curriculum Feature — Mandarin Chinese:** One of the very few schools in Eastern India to offer **Mandarin as a 3rd language option** alongside French and German. Strategically significant for students targeting business or international careers.

### 🧠 Brain Development & Special Activities

**Chess Culture (★★★★★ — National Level)**
Perhaps the most serious chess programme in any Kolkata school. The Moira Street branch has produced national-level and state-level chess champions. Chess is woven into the school's cultural fabric, with inter-house tournaments, dedicated coaching, and students routinely qualifying for CBSE National Chess Championships. The school has produced players who have gone on to earn national ratings.

**Abacus Programme (★★★☆☆ — Integrated Early Learning)**
Abacus is integrated into the early learning modules (primary section) specifically to build mental math speed and number fluency. It is not a standalone SIP/UCMAS partnership but an internal pedagogy choice. Students who show aptitude are encouraged to participate in external Abacus competitions.

**Robotics & Tinkering Lab — STEMROBO Partnership**
Partnered with STEMROBO Technologies to run its tinkering lab. Students work on coding, Arduino-based robots, and participate in national-level competitions. The school has won **First Place in National Science Olympiads** and has 13+ active clubs including Robotics, Astronomy, Literary Society, Debate, and Culinary.

**Astronomy Club:** One of the few schools with a dedicated Astronomy programme — connects to CBSE Science Olympiad preparation and ISRO-linked awareness programmes.

**German Exchange Programme:** Students and teachers travel to Germany (cities including Cuxhaven and Berlin) — a real-world language and cultural immersion that most schools only talk about.

**Other Key Activities:** Elocution, MUN (Model UN), Quizzing, Football, Cricket, Volleyball, Basketball, Chess, Swimming, and Community Service.

**Happiness Quotient:** An independent Skill Tree survey gave BHS a **happiness score of 4.13/5** based on student, parent, and teacher responses — a remarkably high satisfaction metric for a school of this academic intensity.

### 💪 Strengths
- Unmatched legacy alumni network (CEOs, CFOs, IAS officers, CAs, IITians) that actively benefits current students during placements and college counselling
- Strongest Mathematics culture in Kolkata for boys — consistently produces top scorers
- British Council International School Award (received multiple times) — validates global curriculum alignment
- Rare language combination: English + Hindi/Bengali + French/German/Mandarin
- Excellent discipline framework without being militaristic
- Strong co-curricular record — not just an "exam factory"

### ⚠️ Weaknesses & Cautions
- **Boys only at Moira Street** — families with daughters must consider the Mukundapur branch
- High academic pressure, especially from Class IX onwards
- Competitive peer environment that can be unhealthy for some temperament types
- Campus is urban (Moira Street, Central Kolkata) — limited green/open space
- Admission is competitive, particularly at higher classes

## 🏆 SCHOOL 3: The Newtown School (NTS), New Town

> *"India's First IGBC Gold Green School — The School of the Future"*

### 🎯 Identity & Legacy
The Newtown School (NTS) has rapidly carved out a unique identity. It holds the distinction of being **India's first school to receive IGBC Gold Certification** under the Green Schools Rating System — a recognition of its eco-friendly infrastructure including solar panels, rainwater harvesting, and water recycling systems. Located in New Town (Rajarhat), it serves the fastest-growing residential and IT hub in Kolkata.

**Unique Positioning:** NTS is an **Apple Distinguished School** — a designation awarded by Apple Inc. to schools demonstrating exceptional integration of Apple technology into daily learning. Every classroom uses iPads, and the school has iTheatres for presentation-based learning.

### 🥇 Verified Rankings & Awards

| Ranking Body | Year | Position |
|---|---|---|
| Cfore India School Rankings | 2024 | **#2 Co-Ed School in Kolkata** |
| IGBC (Indian Green Building Council) | — | **Gold Certification** — First school in India |
| EducationWorld | — | Regularly featured in top CBSE co-ed lists for WB |
| Ezyschooling | 2026–27 | Listed in Top 6 CBSE schools in Kolkata |

### 📚 CBSE Curriculum & Academic Depth

**Primary:** Apple Coding Curriculum using **Swift Playgrounds**. LEGO Education kits for Robotics and STEM from primary level. Every child is a tablet user from nursery onwards.

**Middle School:** Integration of AI, 3D Design, and Robotics into CBSE curriculum. Visual Math approach using interactive software and tablets. iTheatre-based presentations replace traditional project submission.

**3D Printing Lab (Ultimaker Pro):** Students design and print physical objects — one of very few schools in Kolkata with this capability. Builds spatial reasoning and design thinking from a young age.

**Senior School (IX–XII):** CBSE board with Science, Commerce, and Humanities. Board results improving — 100% pass rate with 30%+ scoring above 90%.

**Languages Offered:** English, Hindi, and a choice of **French, German, or Spanish** — offering Spanish is rare and valuable for global career paths.

**Sports Infrastructure (Best in List):**

- Squash courts
- Rifle Shooting range — exceptionally rare for a school
- Large green sports grounds (benefit of Newtown location)

### 🧠 Brain Development & Special Activities

**Chess (★★★★☆ — Elite External Partnership)**
Formal partnership with the **Dibyendu Barua Chess Academy** — named after GM Dibyendu Barua, one of India's chess legends and a Kolkata icon. Students receive coaching from trained coaches associated with the Academy.

**Abacus (★★★★☆ — Saturday Club Integration)**
Abacus is offered as part of the school's **"Saturday Club"** programme — within school hours, not after-school or external.

**Apple Technology Ecosystem (★★★★★)**
- iPads in every classroom from primary
- Swift Playgrounds for coding
- iTheatre for creative presentations

**LEGO Education Robotics:** Structured LEGO robotics curriculum from primary school — builds algorithmic thinking, teamwork, and engineering mindset.

**Rifle Shooting:** Small bore rifle shooting range — offering national sport shooter development from school level.

- India's first IGBC Gold Green School — strong environmental education component
- Chess coaching through the Dibyendu Barua Academy is elite-level access
- Spanish as a language option — rare and forward-thinking
- 3D Printing + Robotics + Coding (Swift) in one school — unmatched tech toolkit

- Board result track record is still building — 30%+ above 90% is good but not yet at Birla or DPS levels

## 🏆 SCHOOL 4: Delhi Public School (DPS) Ruby Park

> *"The Gold Standard of Results — East India's Most Consistent CBSE Performer"*

Established in **2003**, DPS Ruby Park is the **first DPS school in Eastern India** and the most decorated DPS branch in the region. Part of the DPS Society, New Delhi — one of the largest school networks in India with 200+ campuses worldwide. Located in Ruby Park, South Kolkata.

**Notable Alumni:**

**CBSE Academic Results (Verified):**

- Hundreds of students qualify JEE Mains each academic cycle

### 📚 CBSE Curriculum & Academic Depth

**Curriculum Philosophy:** DPS Ruby Park runs on the DPS Society's national curriculum framework, specifically designed to be **JEE/NEET-aligned** from Class VI onwards.

**Speed Math:** Timed practice, mental calculation drills, and competitive mock papers embedded into Mathematics teaching from primary school.

**AI as a Subject:** Artificial Intelligence offered as a **6th skill subject** from Class VIII — one of the first schools to comply with CBSE's AI curriculum initiative. The approach is more theoretical and exam-oriented rather than hands-on.

**Student:Teacher Ratio:** ~40:1 — the highest of the seven schools.

**Languages:** French and German available as optional third languages.

### 🧠 Brain Development & Special Activities

**Chess (★★★☆☆ — Sports Curriculum)**
Chess is part of the school's sports curriculum but treated as an activity rather than a core skill. Inter-school chess representation happens, but there is no dedicated championship-level coaching track.

**Abacus (★★☆☆☆ — After-School Programme)**
Offered through after-school programmes on campus — optional and not mainstreamed into learning.

**Math Olympiad Culture (★★★★★)**
DPS Ruby Park has a strong Olympiad culture — students regularly clear Round 1 and Round 2 of Mathematics, Science, and Informatics Olympiads. The "Speed Math" pedagogy directly feeds into this. The strongest aspect of brain development at DPS.

**Debate, MUN, and Quiz:** Well-established clubs with wins at multiple inter-school competitions.

### 💪 Strengths
- **Highest CBSE board result density** of the seven schools
- DPS Society brand provides a nationally recognized credential
- Extraordinary alumni achievers at global institutions (MIT, Duke, ISI)
- AI as a subject from Class VIII
- Strongest Maths Olympiad culture

- "Fun" activities progressively deprioritized as board exams approach
- Tech education is exam-oriented rather than creative

## 🏆 SCHOOL 7: South Point High School (SPHS), Ballygunge

> *"71 Years of Stress-Free Excellence — Guinness Record, Nobel Laureate Alumni, and Kolkata's First Co-Ed School"*

### 🎯 Identity & Legacy

South Point is a higher-secondary co-educational private school affiliated to CBSE, consisting of three linked organisations: **South Point School (SPS)** handling Nursery to Class V, **South Point High School (SPHS)** handling Classes VI to XII, and the **South Point Education Society** as the administrative authority. The school was founded in **1954** and was the **first co-educational school in Kolkata**. It entered the **Guinness Book of World Records as the largest educational institute in the world** in 1984, a status it held until 1992.

The school is managed by the **M.P. Birla Group** — the same conglomerate that also manages Birla High School (School 1 in this report) — providing institutional financial stability and governance quality comparable to the best in Kolkata. It is also an **ISO 45001:2018 Certified Institution**, independently audited for student and staff safety systems.

**Important Structural Note for Parents:** A child admitted to SPS in Nursery will naturally progress to SPHS at Class VI — this is a planned, managed transition, not a surprise. Families can also seek direct admission to SPHS from Class VI onwards subject to vacancy.

**Student Strength:** Very large student body (legacy of being the world's largest school); operates in **two shifts** (morning and afternoon) to manage volume. Morning shift is generally preferred by parents.

**Location:** 82/7A Ballygunge Place, Kolkata — 700019 (SPHS campus); Birla Building, 9/1 R.N. Mukherjee Road, Kolkata — 700001 (Administrative Office)

### 📚 CBSE Curriculum & Academic Depth

**Primary — South Point School (SPS, Nursery–V):**

The school's teaching methodology is based on the **theory of Multiple Intelligence** (Howard Gardner). This formally recognizes that children are gifted in different domains — mathematical, linguistic, musical, spatial, kinesthetic, interpersonal, intrapersonal, and naturalistic — and the school's programme is designed accordingly. This is a significantly more sophisticated pedagogical philosophy than most schools, which default to treating exam score as the only measure of ability.

**The Mindspark Programme — Classes II to IV (★★★★★ — Unique & Evidence-Based):**
South Point School uses the **Mindspark** digital adaptive learning platform for Classes II to IV. Mindspark is developed by Educational Initiatives, one of India's most respected EdTech organisations. The platform uses AI to track each child's individual understanding, identifies gaps in real time, and tailors practice to exactly the right level for each student. This is not marketing — Mindspark has published research validating its effectiveness in improving foundational maths and reading skills. This is one of the most genuinely sophisticated and evidence-backed uses of education technology at the primary level across all seven schools.

**Middle & Senior School — SPHS (Classes VI–XII):**

Standard CBSE curriculum across all three streams — Science, Commerce, and Humanities at XI–XII. The school is known for producing strong board result percentages, with **two-thirds of students scoring above 90%** in CBSE boards — matching DPS Ruby Park (ranked #4) in academic output density.

**Dedicated Labs at SPHS:** Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computer Science. Audio-Visual Aid facility for Classes VI–XII (indicating regular AV-based teaching, not occasional use). Computer Laboratory for Classes XI–XII.

**CBSE Board Results:** Two-thirds of students scoring above 90% at fees significantly lower than comparable schools — making this the strongest board result–to–fee ratio of all seven schools researched.

**Counselling Cell:** SPHS has a dedicated Counselling Cell — formal psychological support services for students, relatively rare and genuinely valuable.

## 💡 Practical Admission Tips

**Birla High (Moira Street):** Admission happens primarily at Kindergarten. Higher class vacancies are extremely rare. Parents should apply in the first week of December for the following academic year. Boys-only — check Mukundapur branch for girls.

**LSA:** Admission process involves a lottery system for primary classes (due to extremely high demand), followed by an interaction session. No academic test at early grades. For Class XI (non-LSA students), direct academic admission is possible.

**NTS:** Admission is more straightforward — registration + interaction. Being in New Town gives it lower demand pressure than central Kolkata schools. Best opportunity for tech-oriented families who missed LSA.

**DPS Ruby Park:** Admission at lower classes requires registration + interaction. At senior classes (XI), direct admission is offered based on Class X board marks. Popular for students from other schools who want to join for XI–XII.

**GD Goenka Dakshineswar:** Generally more accessible admission — benefits from being in North Kolkata where competition for premium school seats is lower. Good option for families in Dakshineswar, Baranagar, Sodepur, and Barasat areas.

**BDMI:** Large school with regular admissions throughout the year. Being in Garia makes it accessible. Check for the CBSE section specifically (school may have multiple sections).

**South Point:** Applications for SPS (Nursery) open typically in November–December for the following academic year. SPHS direct admission (Class VI onwards) depends on vacancy after internal promotions from SPS — apply early. The two-shift system means seat availability can vary by shift; morning shift is typically more competitive. Check southpoint.ac.in or call the school office at +91 33 24406208. Online application is available at the official website.
